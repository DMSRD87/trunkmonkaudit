04 — Where Google Can't Find You

Local SEO gaps — same-day fixable.

Three concrete issues blocking your Google rankings. Each one is a same-day or same-week fix, and each one ties directly to your "rank highest" goal.

01

Half your services are invisible to Google.

Your Google Business Profile lists three services publicly: Tree removal, Tree stump removal, Tree trimming and pruning. Your website lists six: those three plus Consulting Arborist, Wood Chipping, and Habitat Installation.

Google ranks GBPs partly on the services you've listed. Anyone searching "consulting arborist Byron Bay" or "habitat installation Mullumbimby" will not find you, even though those are services you offer.

Same-day fix · High ranking impact
02

Your service area copy is too vague to rank locally.

Your website describes your service area as "Northern NSW and SE QLD." Google needs city-level anchors — specific suburb names — to rank you for local searches like "arborist Byron Bay" or "tree removal Mullumbimby."

Tallow Tree Services has a dedicated landing page for every suburb they serve. That's how they show up first when someone in Lennox Head searches for an arborist. You don't have those pages yet, so even when someone searches with strong intent, you're invisible.

Phase 2 fix · Highest ranking impact
03

Your customers are telling Google what to rank you for. Your website isn't listening.

Google has extracted these themes from your reviews — meaning these are the search intents your existing customers describe in their own words:

Dead gum tree removal Lightning struck trees Palm removal Minimise impacts +6 more

None of these specific phrases appear on your website. Each one is a search-intent keyword, ready to be turned into ranking content. This is rare: most clients don't have this data — yours is sitting there for free.

Phase 2 fix · Compounding impact
05 — Where Premium Clients Drop Off

Visibility gets them in. Conversion turns them into paying jobs.

Even if Phase 1 doubles your traffic, the conversion side of the funnel needs work to make sure those visitors turn into the premium quotes you're chasing.

04

The trust on Google isn't on your website.

Your homepage carousel shows three customer testimonials. Anyone landing on your site who hasn't seen your Google profile — say, a referral clicking your URL, or a Facebook visitor — sees a fraction of the proof you've built.

For a business chasing premium clients who research before they call, your 143 five-star reviews need to be the loudest thing on every page.

Phase 2 fix · Direct conversion impact
05

Two typos on your services page.

"consultationg" and "oppourtunities" — both currently visible on the live services page. Small thing, but the premium clients you're targeting notice details, and it's the kind of friction that makes a careful buyer pause.

Same-day fix · Trust signal
06

The current website is built on a platform that limits what we can fix.

Your site is on Wix, which limits the SEO controls and structural changes needed to add proper location pages, schema markup, and the keyword targeting your reviews are pointing toward. To execute the full Phase 2 plan cleanly, the site needs a rebuild on a platform that gives us those levers.

This is also what we did for Craig at EDI Tree & Garden — same Wix-to-modern-platform move, ready to ship faster with a tighter setup.

Phase 1 first. Prove results before you spend more.

Weeks 1–12

Google Business Profile optimisation & management

Unlock the rankings your reputation has already earned. Same-day fixes for the GBP gaps, then ongoing management for compounding results.

  • Add all six services to GBP (Consulting Arborist, Wood Chipping, Habitat Installation + the three already listed)
